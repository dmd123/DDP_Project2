PJM LMP Data

This application is a tool for viewing realtime and filtering electricity pricing for the PJM Interconnection. The PJM Interconnectionn is a regional transmission organization that includes all or parts of Delaware, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Maryland, Michigan, New Jersey, North Carolina, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, Virginia, West Virginia, and the District of Columbia. Its operations include managing the electricity transmission system and operating a wholesale electricity market.

Electricity prices for the preceding 5 minute interval and most recent hour are updated continuously on the PJM website. These prices are known as locational marginal prices, or LMPs. They are provided for thousands of specific delivery locations, which are known as Nodes.
